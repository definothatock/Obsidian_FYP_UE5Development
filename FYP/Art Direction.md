
A good planned out environment and contrasts can drive player interest and world progression. This is why games like Outwilds succeed with only exploration elements. Even our game is procedural generated and small in scale, the gameplay loop leans towards exploration and discovery, thus how the world and environment prorate meanings to the player is important. 

 ***But remember, even Arts are something player need to perceive! they are information too! Their introductions should be progressive, if they have some information volume (players need effort to 'wonder', even they do want to 'wonder').***

Study from related games:
Peak: this game is not about exploration, it is about reaching goal (Peak), thus the world doesn't really contribute too much at telling 'what', but more about telling 'how' (which route to take).
REPO/lethal: Although repetitive after certain loops and very goofy, the game still tried to consistently tell 'what' happened to the world or 'what' it was (different sets of items / environmental damages in different level).

### Genre of world structures
2 main structures in the game that tells 2 very different meanings:
- Geometrical/Brutalist Architectures
	- This reflects the idea of utilitarianism, rules and formations, the de-romantic after industrialization. 
	- Some good reference: brutality Architectures and Soviets era buildings
	- Dominating locations:
		- deep Undergrounds (subways)
		- Strongholds
		- mega structures
- Fungal Overgrown
	- This reflects the corrosive and wildness of nature, the reach of chaos, now synthetically amplified.
	- Some good reference: Combine from HL series and corruption sea in Nausicaa
	- Dominating locations:
		- general surface locations
		- anything that failed to battle the overgrown

1 sub structure:
- some really 'natural' scenery that blends inside the world.
	- This provides player an occasional harmony, to feel the dynamics within the world.
	- Can be something natural that we might be familiar in real life and really enjoy: flower/grass field, well maintained greeneries. 

These categories helps build clashes between consistence 'forces'.
